Information About a Freshman Bible Major at BJU

About a student from the BJU Biblical Studies program 

Bob Jones has the best!

BJU has an excellent Bible major program. Some benefits of the program are caring and knowledgeable faculty, rigorous and solid academics, and supportive and friendly peers. I believe the LORD is leading me to a ministry in His church and believe He has led me to be educated at Bob Jones. 

Living in Spartanburg, South Carolina

Spartanburg is a city in the Upstate of SC. It is more rural than Greenville and has many acres of undeveloped land and farms. I live in the country and enjoy the outdoors. My family and I have some land and chickens that we take care of. 

Cornerstone Baptist Church

I attend Cornerstone Baptist Church on Pelham Road in Greenville. It is a large church of around 600, but, despite the size, we maintain a close, tight-knit, familial community. Our Pastor is Gary Reimers. He is a godly and upright man who fears God and faithfully preaches His Word to our congregation. Additionally, he holds a doctorate from Bob Jones Seminary and also teaches there. It is conservative in music style and maintains a Biblical and reverent worship philosophy. We strive to honor the LORD in all that we do and seek to cultivate a God-honoring and edifying church culture where we serve the LORD together.
